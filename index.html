About

Hello everyone, welcome to my fellowship site.
I am Nicola Hall, I currently work as a Consultant Epidemiologist for the Ministry of Health and Wellness (MOHW) in Jamaica. For the last four years I have been working on the CDC-MOHW Cooperative Agreement. The project aims to strengthen health information systems through the digital transformation process that addresses data quality, protection, and availability. Under this project we have used openEHR standards to develop archetypes and templates for the data collection forms. The electronic disease surveillance system;serves as a vital component of the evolving Clinical Data Repository and Electronic Health Record (EHR) infrastructure.
